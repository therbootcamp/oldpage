The Instructors

We are Dr. Dirk Wulff and Dr. Nathaniel Phillips, two cognitive scientists who have jointly spent over 15 years doing data science with R for both research and industry applications. We have written several R packages including cstab, mousetrap, yarr, FFTrees, taught R courses at the Bachelor’s and PhD level, and written the book YaRrr! The Pirate’s Guide to R.
