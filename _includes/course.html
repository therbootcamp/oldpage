Our goal is to teach you the latest tools and techniques to discover and communicate insights from data in a learning-friendly environment to ready you for the data revolution. Together with our Team of like-minded R enthusiast we offer a diverse collection of Data Science courses in Switzerland and Germany. Currently we offer three standalone courses. Our courses are complementary and can be taken in any order. Beginners with no R or programming background we recommend, however, to first complete Intro to R for Data Science. Intro to R for Data Science teaching you the basics of R and how to use it to explore,, manipulate, and visualize data. Statistics with R teaching you the fundamentals of statistical inferenceand how to R to derive actionable analytics from data. Machine Learning with R teaching you the fundamentals of Machine Learning and how to use R to derive robust predictions from data. Our courses are composed of short, interactive presentations, introducing you to new topics and techniques, and longer hands-on practicals, during which you will work through step-by-step exercises designed to make you and active user of R. For every 5 participants there will be 1 instructor to be able to give you 1:1 support catering to your specific level and needs.
