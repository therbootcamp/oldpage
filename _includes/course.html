R Bootcamps

Learn Data Science from experienced R communicators and developers in a learning-friendly environment. We take the necessary time to teach what you need to become ready for the data revolution.

Our courses teach all aspects of data science with R in in-depth, hands-on courses for beginners, academics, and industry professionals. We cover statistical analysis and machine learning. We show you how to make elegant, informative data visualizations for data both big and small. We introduce you to some of R's most efficient libraries for data analysis and tools to write transparent, reproducible reports.

Feel free to explore the materials of our LATEST COURSE.

Essentials


Get to know the R and RStudio environment. Load your data into R and do basic manipulations.

Analysis


Descriptive and inferential statistics. Applied machine learning and statistical prediction.

Visualisations


Publication-ready visualisations for small and big data. Interactive websites with Shiny.

Productivity


Speed up your code. Write and share reproducible code and reports using R Markdown.
