We teach R

We are Dr. Dirk Wulff and Dr. Nathaniel Phillips, two Basel-based data scientists who have jointly spent over 20 years doing data science with R for both research and industry. We founded The R Bootcamp out of our passion for R and Data Science. Our goal is to teach you in a learning-friendly environment the skills needed to discover and communicate insights from data and ready you for the data revolution. Together with our Team of like-minded R enthusiast we offer a diverse collection of Data Science courses in Switzerland and Germany.

Currently, we offer three standalone courses. Our courses are complementary and can be taken in any order. Beginners with no R or programming background we recommend, however, to first complete Intro to R for Data Science.

R for Data Science
Learn the basics of R and how to use it to explore, manipulate, and visualize data.
 Statistics with R
Learn the basics of Statistics and how to use R to derive actionable analytics from data.
 Machine Learning with R
Learn the fundamentals of Machine Learning and how to use R to generate robust predictions from data.

Our courses are composed of short, interactive presentations, introducing you to new topics and techniques, and longer hands-on practicals, during which you will work through step-by-step exercises designed to make you an active user of R. For every 5 participants there will be 1 instructor to ensure personal support catering to your specific level and needs.

Feel free to explore our open materials of our latest and previous courses. If you have any questions don't hesitate to send us a message. To stay in touch and learn about the latest courses, sign up for our mailing list.
