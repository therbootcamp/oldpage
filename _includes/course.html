We teach R

We are Dr. Dirk Wulff and Dr. Nathaniel Phillips, two Basel-based data scientists who have jointly spent over 20 years doing data science with R for both research and industry. We have taught dozens of courses in R in- and outside of University, written the free, online e-book YaRrr! The Pirate’s Guide to R, and are active R developers, having written several R packages including cstab, memnet, mousetrap, yarrr, and FFTrees.

We founded The R Bootcamp out of our passion for R and Data Science. Our goal is to teach you the latest tools and techniques to discover and communicate insights from data in a learning-friendly environment to ready you for the data revolution. Together with our Team of like-minded R enthusiast we offer a diverse collection of Data Science courses in Switzerland and Germany.

Currently we offer three standalone courses. Our courses are complementary and can be taken in any order. Beginners with no R or programming background we recommend, however, to first complete Intro to R for Data Science.

Intro to R for Data Science
teaching you the basics of R and how to use it to explore, manipulate, and visualize data.
 Statistics with R
teaching you the basics of Statistics and how to use R to derive actionable analytics from data.
 Machine Learning with R
teaching you the fundamentals of Machine Learning and how to use R to generate robust predictions from data.

Our courses are composed of short, interactive presentations, introducing you to new topics and techniques, and longer hands-on practicals, during which you will work through step-by-step exercises designed to make you and active user of R. For every 5 participants there will be 1 instructor to be able to give you 1:1 support catering to your specific level and needs.

Feel free to explore the materials of our latest and previous courses. If you have any questions don't hesitate to send us a message. To stay in touch and learn about the latest courses, sign up for our mailing list.
