We teach R

Learn Data Science from experienced R instructors and developers in a learning-friendly environment. We will show you the latest tools and techniques to discover and communicate insights from data, making you ready for the data revolution.

Our courses teach the full spectrum of data science with R in in-depth, hands-on courses for beginners, academics, and industry professionals. We cover statistical analysis and machine learning. We show you how to make elegant, informative data visualizations for data both big and small. We introduce you to some of R's most efficient libraries for data analysis and tools to write transparent, reproducible reports.

Feel free to explore the materials of our LATEST COURSE.
