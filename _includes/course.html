We teach R

We are Dr. Dirk Wulff and Dr. Nathaniel Phillips, two Basel-based data scientists who have jointly spent over 20 years doing data science with R for both research and industry. We founded The R Bootcamp out of our passion for R and Data Science. Together with our Team of like-minded R enthusiasts we host public and in-house Data Science courses around Switzerland and Germany. Our goal is to help you learn the skills needed to discover and communicate insights from data.

Currently, we offer three standalone, complementary courses. For beginners with no R or other programming experience we recommend to first complete R for Data Science. For detailed course description see Upcoming Bootcamps.

R for Data Science
Learn the basics of R and how to use it to explore, manipulate, and visualize data.
 Statistics with R
Learn to derive actionable analytics from data using statistical inference in R. 		Machine Learning with R
Learn about the power of Machine Learning and how to employ it with R.

Our courses are composed of short, interactive presentations and longer hands-on practicals, during which you will work through step-by-step exercises designed to make you an active user of R. For every 5 participants there will be 1 instructor to ensure personalized support catering to your specific level and needs. Feel free to explore our open materials of our latest and previous courses. If you have any questions don't hesitate to send us a message. To learn about the latest courses, sign up for our mailing list.
