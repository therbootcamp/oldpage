About Us

We Teach R.

We are Dr. Dirk Wulff and Dr. Nathaniel Phillips, two scientists who have jointly spent over 20 years doing data science with R for both research and industry applications. We have written several R packages including cstab, mousetrap, yarrr, FFTrees, taught R courses at the Bachelor’s and PhD level, and authored the book YaRrr! The Pirate’s Guide to R.

If you have any questions don't hesitate to send us a message. To stay in touch and learn about the latest courses, sign up for our mailing list.
