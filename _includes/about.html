Team


Dr. Dirk Wulff
Founder
    

Dirk is a researcher at the University of Basel and the Max Planck Institute for Human Development, Berlin, specialising in decision science and mathematical models of cognition. He is an expert on big data applications in the behavioral sciences using R, Python, and C++. He is an active R developer having authored the packages cstab, memnet, mousetrap.


Dr. Nathaniel Phillips
Founder
  

Nathaniel is a data and decision scientist who specialises in machine learning, statistical inference, and R. Currently, he is a data scientist at Roche, where he leverages big data, machine learning, and visualisations to further the vision of personalised health care. He has authored the free, online e-book YaRrr! The Pirate’s Guide to R and the R packages yarrr, and FFTrees.


MSc Markus Steiner
Team Basel
    

Markus is a PhD student at the University of Basel. He studies risky decisions and specialises in building computational and statistical models of human cognition, using these to predict and improve real life decisions.


Dr. Michael Schulte-Mecklenbeck
Team Bern
  

Michael is a senior lecturer for methodology at the department of Business Administration, University of Bern. His research focuses human decision processes, eye-tracking and real world traffic behavior. He is an experienced user of R and organises the BernR meetup.
