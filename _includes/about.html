About Us

We Teach R.

We are Dr. Dirk Wulff and Dr. Nathaniel Phillips, two data scientists who have jointly spent over 20 years doing data science with R for both research and industry applications. We founded The R Bootcamp out of our passion for teaching and communicating R. We have taught dozens of courses in R and statistics at the Bachelor’s and PhD level, and have written the free, online e-book YaRrr! The Pirate’s Guide to R. We are active R developers and have written several R packages including cstab, mousetrap, yarrr, and FFTrees.

If you have any questions don't hesitate to send us a message. To stay in touch and learn about the latest courses, sign up for our mailing list.

Team.
